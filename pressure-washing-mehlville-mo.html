Why Mehlville homes need exterior cleaning

In Mehlville, we commonly see a combination of mature neighborhoods, established landscaping, heavy seasonal pollen, and Missouri humidity creating ideal conditions for exterior staining. Many Mehlville homes have decades of exposure to the elements — that doesn’t mean they’re poorly maintained, it simply means exterior surfaces have had years to accumulate contaminants.

Algae growth

Green staining is one of the most common issues affecting siding, fences, retaining walls, and concrete. Algae thrives in shaded, damp environments — often first noticed on north-facing walls or areas protected by landscaping.

Pollen accumulation

Spring can leave a surprising amount of buildup on siding, soffits, windows, and outdoor surfaces. Over time, pollen combines with dirt and moisture for a dingy appearance.

Moisture retention

Tree cover and landscaping often keep surfaces from drying completely after rain and morning dew — favorable conditions for mildew and organic growth.

Everyday dirt

Even well-maintained homes gradually accumulate dirt, dust, and environmental debris. The change is usually so gradual homeowners stop noticing it.

Driveway cleaning in Mehlville

Driveways are often the first surface homeowners notice after cleaning — concrete shows years of buildup very clearly. Most driveways are exposed to dirt, algae, tire residue, organic debris, leaf staining, and weather exposure. Many homeowners assume the discoloration is permanent. Often, it isn’t.

Professional cleaning is highly effective at removing surface dirt that hides concrete’s original color, organic growth from algae and mildew, general environmental discoloration, and leaf staining. What it cannot fix: large cracks, settling, structural damage, or severe spalling. If replacement is ultimately the better solution, we’ll tell you.

House washing in Mehlville

House washing is one of the highest-impact exterior improvements homeowners can make. Many Mehlville homes develop visible siding staining long before homeowners realize what’s causing it — green algae, black streaking, spider webs, dirt accumulation, pollen buildup, and general discoloration. The good news is that most of these issues respond extremely well to professional cleaning.

Why soft washing matters

Many homeowners use “pressure washing” to describe all exterior cleaning, but many homes benefit from soft washing instead — specialized cleaning solutions and controlled, low-pressure application designed to safely remove organic growth. For many homes, this is safer and more effective than simply increasing pressure.

House washing covers vinyl siding, brick, stucco, fiber cement siding, soffits, fascia, and trim — every surface needs a different approach, which is why a one-size-fits-all method doesn’t work.

Patio and sidewalk cleaning

Patios and sidewalks are easy to overlook because homeowners walk past them every day. Over time they accumulate algae, dirt, leaf staining, and moisture-related discoloration. Cleaning can dramatically improve outdoor living spaces while helping define walkways and gathering areas — many homeowners are surprised by the difference once years of buildup are gone.

Is pressure washing worth it?

For many homeowners, yes. It’s often one of the most noticeable improvements available without a major renovation — a clean property can look brighter, better maintained, and more inviting. The key is understanding what cleaning can and can’t do: it removes buildup, but it doesn’t reverse age, repair damage, or make worn materials new again. Even so, many homeowners are surprised by how much improvement proper cleaning alone can achieve.

Frequently asked questions

How much does pressure washing cost in Mehlville?

Most residential projects fall between $75 and $900 depending on the service, surface size, and condition.

Why does my driveway look darker than it used to?

Usually it’s gradual surface buildup — dirt, algae, and organic residue — rather than permanent staining or aging concrete.

Is soft washing different from pressure washing?

Yes. Soft washing uses lower pressure and specialized solutions to safely remove organic growth from siding, while pressure washing targets durable surfaces like concrete.

How often do Mehlville homes need exterior cleaning?

Most homes benefit from cleaning every one to three years, with shaded, tree-covered properties often needing it more frequently.

Can cleaning fix cracked or settling concrete?

No. Cleaning improves appearance but doesn’t repair structural issues. If replacement is the better option, we’ll tell you.

Will cleaning remove green algae from siding?

In most cases, yes — algae and mildew respond well to professional soft washing.
